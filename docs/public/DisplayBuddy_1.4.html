DisplayBuddy 1.4

  • DisplayBuddy now supports Apple Silicon (M1) Macs!
  • DisplayBuddy works on M1 macs using software emulation: it modifies the display output from your Mac instead of directly changing the monitor's settings.
  • You can now click on your display name to:
    • Change your display name
    • Customise the Brightness, Contrast and Volume sliders
    • Change how DisplayBuddy controls your display -> DDC / Apple Display Protocol / Software Control
